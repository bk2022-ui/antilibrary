## Key concepts and design decisions

### The taxonomy (3 levels)
1. **Category** тАФ 19 broad (Philosophy, Design, Science, etc.) тАФ stored as `b.category`
2. **Subject** тАФ 60 BISAC-aligned mid-level (e.g. "eastern-philosophy-zen") тАФ stored as `b.subject` (slug), displayed via subjects.json
3. **Spine** тАФ 17 intellectual threads that cut across categories (invisible connections a reader sees) тАФ stored in analysis.json

### View modes
- **Grid** тАФ default, all books with covers (2:3 portrait ratio, Open Library API)
- **By Spine** тАФ books grouped under their intellectual thread with dropdown selector
- **Clusters** тАФ curated thematic groups (Japan is the first)

### Statuses
- `read` тАФ read
- `antilibrary` тАФ owned, not yet read
- `to-buy` тАФ want to buy
- `misplaced` тАФ owned but can't find (new тАФ added for Designing Design by Kenya Hara)

### Book covers
Open Library Cover API: `https://covers.openlibrary.org/b/id/{id}-L.jpg`
Stored as `coverUrl` on each book. Toggle button in UI.

### Lego blocks (ideas.json)
Each book has 3тАУ5 atomic, transferable ideas. Format:
```json
{"Book Title": [{"title": "Short plain name", "gloss": "1-2 sentences. Exact mechanism. No decoration."}, ...]}
```
Style rule: direct, specific, no wordplay. Think Musk тАФ what is the exact idea.
**676/692 done.** 125 missing books being generated by a background agent тАФ check scratchpad for `p8_missing.json` when done, then merge into ideas.json.

### Clusters
`/src/data/clusters.json` тАФ array of `{id, name, description, color, books[]}`.
Japan cluster has 23 books. More clusters planned (user mentioned "a cluster for you" = AI/tech).

## What "spines" are

Spines are NOT subject categories. They are intellectual threads тАФ invisible connections that a reader who has read broadly starts to see across books in different fields. Each spine has a `question`, optional `tension`, optional `anchor`, and a `color`. Books belong to multiple spines. Examples:
- "Darwin's Long Shadow" тАФ evolution as explanatory frame
- "The Stoic Wager" тАФ how to act under uncertainty
- "The Zen Current" тАФ attention, presence, stillness

---

## What "Lego blocks" are

3тАУ5 atomic ideas per book. The idea is that each block is transferable тАФ it can be lifted from its source book and combined with ideas from completely different books. Style: exact mechanism, no decoration. These will eventually power a "what ideas connect these books" feature.
